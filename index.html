All About OLR

What is the story behind Our Lady of the Rosary? The story of the feast of Our Lady of the Rosary focuses on the intercessory power of Mary. It shows that when Christians are in danger, they can go to Mary. And when an individual is in pain, discouraged, or having trouble accepting God's will, he or she can also go to Mary.How do you celebrate the Feast of Our Lady of the Rosary? Pope Gregory XIII later changed the name to the Feast of Our Lady of the Rosary. Ideas for celebrating this feast day at home: Our Lady at Fatima said: “I am the Lady of the Rosary. Continue always to pray the Rosary every day.” Today, Pray the Rosary with your family.
